Stafallen
Starfallen is a top-down 2D vertically scrolling bullet hell shooter video game that puts the player in control of a starship fighting against an invading alien fleet. Pilots must manuever their starcraft through the world collecting different weapons, power-ups, and special abilities to assist them in their fight. The game is currently under development by myself and two other individuals. The game will be written in GameMaker Studio 2.
